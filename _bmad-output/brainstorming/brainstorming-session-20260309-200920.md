## Initial Concept Analysis & User Decisions

**Strengths:**
- **Socratic Method:** Prevents the "shortcut" trap and creates actual value for parents and educators.
- **Multimodal Integration:** Using Gemini Live with Vision is a perfect showcase for a Google Hackathon.
- **Real-time Interaction:** Natural voice conversation (interruptions allowed) mimics a real tutor flow.

**Decisions:**
1. **The "Wait" State:** The agent will **stay silent** during long pauses to give the student space to think.
2. **Vision Strategy:** We will use **"Snapshot" triggers** (e.g., student says "Look at this part") for the POC to manage complexity and latency.
3. **Subject-Specific Personalities:** Initial approval to have the tutor's personality adapt to the subject (Math vs. Science vs. English).
